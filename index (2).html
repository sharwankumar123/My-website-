Mahan Purvajon ki Gaurav Gatha

1. Raja Harishchandra (Satya ka Prateek)

Unhone satya ki raksha ke liye apna rajya, patni aur putra tak ka tyaag kar diya. Unka jeevan sikhata hai ki paristhiti chahe kitni bhi kathin ho, satya ka marg nahi chhodna chahiye.

2. Raja Sagar (Samudra ke Janak)

Inke naam se hi 'Sagar' shabd bana. Inke 60,000 putron ke uddhaar ke liye hi Raghuvansh mein Ganga ka aagman hua.

3. Raja Bhagirath (Parishram ki Parakashta)

Ganga ko dharti par laane ka asambhav karya inhi ke 'Bhagirath Prayas' se safal hua. Inke karan hi aaj Ganga ko Bhagirathi kaha jata hai.

4. Maharaj Prithu (Dharti ke Swami)

Inhone hi sabse pehle kheti aur sabhyata ki neenv rakhi. Dharti ko upjau banaya aur manav jaati ka palan-poshan kiya, isliye is grah ko 'Prithvi' kaha jata hai.

5. Raja Dilip (Gau-Bhakti ke Adarsh)

Raja Dilip (Dwitiya) ne Nandini gaay ki raksha ke liye apne pranon ki baazi laga di thi. Inhi ki ghor tapasya aur punyakarma ke fal-swaroop inke vansh mein aage chalkar Bhagwan Ram ne janm liya.

#60 महाराजा रघु Mahan
